About me

I am an interaction designer and developer, who is extremely enthusiastic about the blockchain ecosystem and software development. I work with Front-End technologies - such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I am constantly learning frontend and blockchain technologies, such as Solidity, to understand secure smart contracts and grow as a Fullstack Javascript/Blockchain developer.

I enjoy building websites for creatives, friends and family, and as a freelancer. Establishing a digital presence in modern society is crucial for marketing your brand or oneself, so I plan to help those in need and create digital voice's for those who do not have one.
